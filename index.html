NeON
BMI Calculator



What is this?

This is a fun and stylish BMI (Body Mass Index) calculator designed with changeable neon themes that glow with vibrant colors.
The neon lights add a touch of excitement and modernity to the traditional BMI calculations.

Neon Themes?

Explore different neon themes by clicking on the neon element boxes located in the background.
Each "neon" theme will change the appearance of the calculator, adding an extra layer of visual excitement to your BMI calculations!
